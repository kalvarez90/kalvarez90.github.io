ABOUT ME

I'm a stay-at-home mom of four children between the ages of 12 to 1 year old. We are a year-round homeschool family. I love encouraging my children always to try something new so they can find their many talents. We are also a pet fostering family, where we get to help our local humane society by caring for dogs or kittens who need a temporary home. We are currently fostering two kittens who had panleukopenia (a feline virus that kills over 95% of kittens). But because we chose to continue care, we were able to save them! I am currently a student working toward a Bachelors' in Web Development at Bellevue University. I have learned HTML and CSS, and I'm currently taking JavaScript and Node courses. I worked at a hospital as a Nursing Tech for about three years before deciding to stay home after I got pregnant with our second child. Since then, I've worked on and off as a Research Manager with a Ph. D. doctor at the hospital I used to work. Now, I'm looking forward to completing my degree in web development at Bellevue University.
